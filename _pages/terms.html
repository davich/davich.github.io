Terms & Conditions for Dealings with David Carlin

Effective: always. Retroactive where applicable.

1. Communication

  • No noreply@ addresses. Emails sent from a noreply@ address will not be read, actioned, or acknowledged. If you want a response, send from an address that can receive one.

  • Compensation for wasted time. Parties found to have materially wasted David Carlin's time may be liable for compensation at a rate commensurate with the severity and duration of the waste. This is not a joke.
  • Meetings must have an agenda. Any meeting requested without a stated purpose and agenda may be declined without explanation.

3. Requests

  • "Can you just…" is a warning sign. Tasks prefaced with "can you just" are rarely just anything. They will be scoped, estimated, and treated accordingly.
  • Deadlines must be disclosed upfront. Revealing a deadline at the eleventh hour does not create an obligation to meet it.

4. Respect

  • Basic courtesy is non-negotiable. Rudeness, condescension, and bad faith are grounds for immediate termination of any engagement, with no obligation to explain or provide notice.

5. Unauthorised Charges

  • Charging without authority is theft. Any charge made to a card, account, or direct debit without explicit, current authorisation — including overcharging beyond an agreed amount — will be treated as theft of funds, not an administrative error.
  • Immediate repayment required. Upon notification, the full unauthorised amount must be returned promptly. "We'll look into it" is not repayment.
  • Compensation scales with time. The longer the funds are held, the greater the compensation owed. Specifically:

    • 8–30 days: full refund + 50% of the amount taken
